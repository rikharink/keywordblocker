    Block Options

    Here you can configure the specific action that should be taken on a given page when a video matches a keyword or channel on your blocklists. Block displays an overlay over the video but doesn't remove it from the results. Remove does remove the video but this can cause a big performance degredation because YouTube keeps loading new videos after removal. So some caution is necessary with the remove option.

    Block Dialog

    Here you can configure the appearence of the block dialog that occurs on a video page that matches a keyword or channel.
